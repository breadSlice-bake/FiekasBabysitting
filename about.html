About Fi3ka's Babysitting Service

My name is Shafieka, and I am a dedicated and caring babysitter based in Blue Downs, Cape Town. I have relevant nanny and childcare training, along with hands-on experience that has helped me build a calm, nurturing, and structured environment for children of all ages.

As a mother of two children myself, I understand how important it is for parents to feel confident and at ease when leaving their children in someone else's care. I treat every child as if they were my own, ensuring they feel safe, supported, and loved at all times.

I focus on creating a positive routine that includes age-appropriate activities, playtime, and gentle guidance, while always prioritizing each child's individual needs. Whether you need occasional babysitting or more regular care, you can trust that your child will be looked after with patience, responsibility, and genuine care.

At Fieka's Babysitting Services, my goal is to give parents peace of mind and children a safe, happy space to grow and thrive.
