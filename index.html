Welcome to Fieka's Babysitting Service

Caring for your child isn't just a job—it's a responsibility we take seriously. Fieka's Babysitting Service provides a safe, reliable, and nurturing environment where your little one can feel comfortable, supported, and understood. Parents deserve peace of mind, and children deserve care that is calm, patient, and consistent. Our goal is to offer both.

Why Parents Choose Us

We focus on creating a space where children feel secure, encouraged, and engaged. Every booking is handled with professionalism, clear communication, and genuine care. Whether you need a few hours of support or full-day supervision, we're here to make life a little easier while giving your child the attention they deserve.

Our Packages

Standard Drop-In Care Flexible, short-term babysitting for parents who need dependable help for a few hours.

Half-Day Care

Full-Day Care

A complete day of supervision, activities, and child-focused guidance in a safe and caring environment.
